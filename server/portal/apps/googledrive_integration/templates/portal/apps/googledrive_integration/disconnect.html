Disconnect Google Drive

By disconnecting your Google Drive account from this portal, you will no longer be able to browse files from Google Drive {{ settings.PORTAL_NAMESPACE }}. This will not delete any files from either this portal or Google Drive. What will happen is that you will be removing the authorization you granted {{ settings.PORTAL_NAMESPACE }} to manage files and folders in your Google Drive account.

For further information on usage, see the Privacy Policy.

To reiterate, this will not delete any files. If you wish to delete the files from either your Google Drive storage or your this portal's storage you will need to do that manually.
